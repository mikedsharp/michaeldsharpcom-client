About the guy in the circle above, looking at you...

Hi there, my name is Mike, and I’m a software developer (currently a Front-end Web Developer) based in North East England.

I’ve been writing applications, games and building websites since I was about 14 years old and I’m profoundly interested in technology and what makes those apps and sites we all use every day ‘tick’; In my mind, programming software is what dreams are made of, and I find building technical solutions to problems infinitely rewarding.

Summary of skills

I’ve been developing web applications collectively for over 3 years professionally (including industrial placements and permanent positions), but have been developing as a hobby and academically for well over 10 years.

In terms of programming languages I primarily work with JavaScript (and its derivatives, such as TypeScript), HTML, CSS (particularly SASS, because it’s awesome!), but on a purely hobbyist level I can write C++ (this was my first language, and my go-to language for writing games, check out my GitHub for some examples!), I have also had some professional and academic experience with MSSQL, I also wrote Java desktop and JavaME applications during university.

In terms of frameworks and libraries, I mostly work with AngularJS (1 and 2...why, this very site has been written in Angular 2 and Typescript!), but I have used JQuery quite extensively in the past both professionally and as a hobbyist. I’m always exploring new technologies in order to improve efficiency, workflow...and well, to learn and just do things in the best way I can!
